TThe Dodge Charger R/T is a coupé with 2 doors and a front mounted engine which transmits the power to the rear wheels. The power is produced by a naturally aspirated engine of 7.2 litre capacity. This powerplant features overhead valve valve gear, a 90 degree V 8 cylinder layout, and 2 valves per cylinder. It develops 390 bhp (395 PS/291 kW) of power at 4700 rpm, and maximum torque of 665 N·m (490 lb·ft/67.8 kgm) at 3200 rpm. Power is delivered to the wheels by means of a 4 speed manual gearbox.
